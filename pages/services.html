Our Services

At G-Mash Garage, we provide a complete range of automotive services to keep your car in excellent condition. From routine maintenance to advanced diagnostics and premium detailing, our certified technicians are here to give your vehicle the care it deserves.

1. Routine Maintenance

Regular maintenance ensures your vehicle runs efficiently and avoids costly breakdowns. Our routine services are quick, affordable, and designed to extend the life of your car.

  • Oil & Filter Change: Protects your engine and improves fuel efficiency.
  • Tyre Rotation & Balancing: Promotes even tyre wear and improves handling.
  • Brake Inspection: Ensures your braking system is safe and responsive.
  • Fluid Checks: Includes coolant, brake fluid, transmission fluid, and power steering fluid.

2. Repair Services

Our expert mechanics handle everything from minor fixes to major engine repairs. We use advanced diagnostic tools to identify problems quickly and repair them efficiently.

  • Engine Repairs: From timing belts to complete engine overhauls.
  • Transmission Repairs: Automatic and manual transmission servicing.
  • Brake System Repairs: Pads, discs, rotors, and hydraulic systems.
  • Suspension & Steering: Shock absorbers, struts, and alignment.
  • Electrical Systems: Battery, alternators, starter motors, and wiring issues.

3. Vehicle Diagnostics

Modern vehicles are equipped with advanced computer systems. Our state-of-the-art diagnostic tools quickly identify issues, saving you time and money.

  • Engine Light Diagnostics: Identify causes of warning lights.
  • Performance Testing: Ensure your car is running at optimal efficiency.
  • Emission Testing: Comply with environmental standards.

4. Premium Services

For car enthusiasts and those who want to give their vehicles extra care, our premium services offer advanced treatments and upgrades.

  • Car Detailing: Interior and exterior deep cleaning for a showroom finish.
  • Paint Protection: Waxing, polishing, and ceramic coating.
  • Performance Tuning: ECU remapping and tuning for improved power and efficiency.
  • Custom Upgrades: Exhausts, suspension kits, and lighting enhancements.

5. Fleet & Corporate Services

Businesses rely on reliable vehicles. Our fleet services are tailored for companies that need their cars, vans, or trucks running without disruption.

  • Scheduled Fleet Maintenance: Keep your business vehicles on the road.
  • Priority Repairs: Fast turnaround times to minimise downtime.
  • Flexible Service Plans: Customised packages for your company’s needs.

6. Emergency Roadside Assistance

Breakdowns can happen anytime. Our emergency team is on call to provide roadside help, ensuring you’re never stranded for long.

  • Jump Starts: Get back on the road quickly.
  • Towing Services: Safe transport to the nearest G-Mash Garage branch.
  • On-Site Repairs: Minor fixes done on the spot.
